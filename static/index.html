Workflow

From zero to app store in three steps

No local tooling. No configuration files. Just your browser.

1

Design visually

Open the dashboard, pick components from the palette, and arrange them on the canvas. The props panel gives you full control over every element.

2

Configure your app

Set up bottom-tab navigation, choose a color theme, enable push notifications, connect analytics, and install SDKs — all from one dashboard.

3

Ship to users

Hit publish. Apt runs an EAS cloud build. Download the IPA or APK, or push over-the-air updates whenever you need.
